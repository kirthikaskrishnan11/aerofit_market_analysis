# Aerofit Market Analysis

## Overview
Business case analysis of customer demographics and product purchasing behavior.

## Objective
To understand customer segments and product preference patterns.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Seaborn

## Key Findings
- Specific age groups dominate product purchases.
- Income level influences product selection.
- Certain customer segments show higher conversion rates.

## Outcome
Provided data-driven insights to support targeted marketing strategies.
